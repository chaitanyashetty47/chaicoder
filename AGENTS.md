## Learned User Preferences

- For presentation decks and case studies, prefer concise 1-slide summaries optimized for 2-3 minute verbal explanations.
- In case study slides, use the right side for visual proof (product/dashboard screenshots) rather than repeating founder testimonials.
- Avoid highlighting stagnant metrics (e.g., "unchanged traffic"); focus on positive business impacts like revenue per visitor or conversion lift.

## Learned Workspace Facts

- The brand color system uses `oklch` values: `--color-chai-foam` (background), `--color-primary-orange` (headlines/glow), `--color-text-dark` (body text), and `--color-teal` (CTAs/accents).
- Case studies (`.mdx` files in `case-study/`) follow a specific structure: Frontmatter (with logo/testimonial), The Challenge, The Solution, Results, and Final Reflection.
- Case study frontmatter supports `clientLogo` and `testimonial.avatar` fields for rendering images.
