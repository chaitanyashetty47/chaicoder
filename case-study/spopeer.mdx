## The Challenge

The sports ecosystem lacks a structured professional identity layer.

**Athletes rely on:**

* Instagram for visibility
* DMs for networking
* Informal referrals for opportunities

**Coaches and clubs:**

* Struggle with discovery
* Lack structured scouting tools
* Have no centralized professional network

There was no platform purpose-built for:

* Professional sports identity
* Cross-role networking
* Performance visibility
* Sponsor & opportunity discovery

The client wanted to build the global network for sports professionals - not another generic social app.


---

## The Solution

We architected Spopeer as a production-ready social platform using:

* **Next.js** (Frontend)
* **Express** (Backend)
* **Supabase + Prisma** (Database & Auth)

**Built in structured phases:**

### Phase 1 – Waitlist & Validation (2 Weeks)

* Landing page launch
* Paid ad traffic routing
* Waitlist capture system
* Invite-only onboarding preparation

**Result:** 5,000+ users captured before beta access.

### Phase 2 – Core Platform Build (3 Months)

We built a full social networking infrastructure:

* Role-based profile system (Athlete / Coach / Club / Supportive Professional)
* Global feed + Following feed
* Image Posting + Like/Comment feature
* Direct messaging
* Real-time notifications
* Admin dashboard
* Analytics tracking

---

## Results (Beta Phase)

**Before public launch:**

* 5,000+ waitlist users
* 3,000 beta users onboarded
* 500 profiles created in first 10 days
* 1,000+ posts created
* 7-minute average session duration
* Strong early adoption across Europe & North America

Invite-only onboarding helped maintain quality and relevance within the network.

---

## Operational Structure

* Role-based data isolation
* Verified profile system
* Admin visibility dashboard
* Structured onboarding control (invite-only)

The goal was not just engagement - but controlled ecosystem growth.

---

## Growth Strategy Layer

**Instead of launching publicly:**

* Built a landing page first
* Ran ads to build waitlist
* Launched invite-only beta
* Prioritized Europe & North America
* Focused on athletes, coaches & clubs first

This allowed demand validation before full release.
