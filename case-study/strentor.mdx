## The Challenge

**Before working together:**

* Generic landing page
* Weak messaging & unclear positioning
* Low conversion flow
* No SEO structure
* No scalable client management system

**Internally:**

* Trainers used Excel + WhatsApp
* Zoom scheduling was manual
* Subscription upgrades/downgrades were manual
* No centralized subscription intelligence

They had ~30,000 monthly visitors from ads - but lacked the infrastructure to scale efficiently.

They didn't need a redesign.

**They needed systems.**

---

## The Solution

We rebuilt Strentor as a **full-stack product ecosystem** using **Next.js + Supabase**, combining product strategy, UX, backend architecture, and automation.

### Phase 1 - Conversion Foundation (Month 1)

* High-converting landing page
* SEO-optimized service pages
* Clear headline / CTA structure
* Mandatory lead capture before Calendly booking

**Result:** Improved conversion tracking and ad efficiency from day one.

### Phase 2 - Core Product System (Months 2-4)

* Client dashboards (Fitness / Psychological / Manifestation)
* Trainer dashboards
* Razorpay subscription engine
* Zoom + Calendly integration
* Role-based system (Client / Trainer / Admin)

**Result:** Automated subscription control and scalable client management.

### Phase 3 - Optimization & Intelligence (Months 5-6)

* Workout tracking engine
* Progress analytics
* BMI/BMR calculators
* Advanced admin panel
* UX improvements from early feedback

**Followed by 4 months of structured scaling support.**

---



## Results (8 Months Post-Rebuild)

* **$5M in revenue generated**
* **30,000 → 100,000+ monthly users**
* **100+ booked discovery calls per month**
* **70+ closed deals in a single month**

Strentor evolved from a generic landing page into a scalable, revenue-aligned wellness infrastructure.

## Conversion Optimization Layer

We discovered abandoned discovery calls weren't being tracked.

**We added:**

* Mandatory Name + Email before booking
* Automated follow-up system

**Recovered 1.9% of abandoned call leads** - meaningful at high-ticket pricing.

## Key Technical Architecture (Built for Scale)

Strentor's pricing model required handling:

* 3 service categories
* Monthly / Quarterly / Annual plans
* Upgrades & downgrades
* Simultaneous subscriptions
* All-in-one bundled plans

**We implemented:**

* Centralized subscription state manager
* Razorpay webhook synchronization
* Backend-truth architecture (frontend reads only)
* Supabase row-level security policies

This ensured subscription consistency, secure role-based access, and clean scaling.
